What is RiverMind?

RiverMind is a retreat designed for those who need space — mentally, spiritually, emotionally. Tucked into nature, we offer calm, quiet, and the freedom to just be. Whether you're seeking reflection or just a break from the noise, this is your place.

Activites & Events

RiverMind is all about making sure guests are able to experience numerous activities outdoors from workouts to adventures. Everyday is meant to be a different experience, a different vibe, while at the same time making sure there is time left at the end of the day for you to whatever of your choosing. RiverMind consist but not limited to: lake swimming, Yoga, paddle boarding, boxing, biking, jogging, hanging out with animals, late night karaoke for those in the vibe, board games, etc... We love our guest and want to make them feel comfortable.
